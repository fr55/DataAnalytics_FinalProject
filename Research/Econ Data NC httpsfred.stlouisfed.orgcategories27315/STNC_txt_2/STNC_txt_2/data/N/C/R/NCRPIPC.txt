Title:               Real Per Capita Personal Income for North Carolina
Series ID:           NCRPIPC
Source:              U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis
Release:             Real Personal Income for States and Metropolitan Areas
Seasonal Adjustment: Not Seasonally Adjusted
Frequency:           Annual
Units:               Chained 2009 Dollars
Date Range:          2008-01-01 to 2016-01-01
Last Updated:        2018-05-17 9:51 AM CDT
Notes:               Real per capita personal income is the real personal income divided by
                     midyear population. Real personal income is personal income at RPPs
                     divided by the national PCE price index. It is estimated for states,
                     state metro/nonmetro portions, metropolitan statistical areas, and the
                     combined nonmetropolitan portion of the United States.
